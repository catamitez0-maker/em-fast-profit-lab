# ReturnReply Pro Sample Delivery Brief

## What Was Reviewed

Four anonymized return requests were checked against a 30-day return window, item condition, value, and reason.

## Recommended Actions

- Approve the size-related exchange and ask for preferred replacement size.
- Route worn-item refund requests to manual inspection before refund approval.
- Treat damaged arrival as urgent and offer replacement or credit after photo confirmation.
- Review late-delivery cases as policy exceptions and prefer store credit if approved.

## Buyer Value

This output gives the support lead a working queue, customer-ready replies, and margin-saving alternatives without buying a full returns platform.

After the $149 pilot, offer $199/month maintenance for weekly return queue cleanup, reply updates, and exchange/store-credit reporting.
