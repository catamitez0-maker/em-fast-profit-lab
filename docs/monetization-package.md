### Objections

**Is this fake-review software?**  
No. It only helps respond to real customer feedback and request honest reviews from real customers.

**Can I edit the replies?**  
Yes. The app creates drafts and keeps the owner in control.

**Do I need to connect Google?**  
No for the first version. Paste reviews or upload a CSV, then export final replies.

## First 7-Day Launch Plan

Day 1: choose one niche and city, prepare 30 prospects.  
Day 2: run each prospect's reviews through the MVP and save 3 sample replies.  
Day 3: send 30 emails plus 30 Instagram/Facebook DMs.  
Day 4: follow up with a 60-second screen recording for the best 10 prospects.  
Day 5: deliver one discounted beta sprint for testimonial.  
Day 6: publish before/after examples with anonymized reviews.  
Day 7: pitch 3 local SEO agencies with the white-label CSV export angle.
