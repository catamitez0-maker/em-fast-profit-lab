# Fast-Profit Project Portfolio

This workspace now treats ReviewSpark Local as project 01 and adds four more quick-monetization projects. The filter for inclusion is simple: visible pain, clear paying user, low integration burden, and a service-wrapper path to revenue before a full SaaS build.

## Evidence Summary

- Home services: Service Experts/Talker Research survey coverage reported many U.S. homeowners are staying put longer, delaying repairs, and need access to trusted professionals. Harvard JCHS reporting also projects U.S. home improvement and maintenance spending above $500B into 2026.
- Restaurants: Axios coverage of the James Beard Foundation/Deloitte industry report says independent restaurants have hit a pricing ceiling and need smarter operations, not broad price hikes.
- Invoices: Xero-related reporting shows late payments remain a material small business cash-flow issue, including half of payments to Australian small businesses being late in one 2025 report and UK payment delays rising in Xero data.
- Short-term rentals: Airbnb says response rate affects Superhost status and search placement, and Business Insider reported hosts using messaging automation to run profitable rentals with minimal monthly time.
