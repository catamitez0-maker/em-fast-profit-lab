# Market Round 2: High-Demand Single-Product MVPs

Decision frame: find 3-5 products with visible market demand, a single narrow job, low integration burden, and a service-led path to first revenue.

## Selected Products

| # | Project | Buyer | Single Product | First Offer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 06 | Chargeback Evidence Kit | Ecommerce merchants | One dispute evidence packet | $149 per packet |
| 07 | ReturnReply Pro | Retail/ecommerce support teams | One return decision and reply queue | $99 setup |
| 08 | AltText Cataloger | Ecommerce catalog teams | One alt-text CSV pass | $129 per 100 images |
| 09 | ChurnSave Script | Subscription businesses | One cancellation-save script pack | $149 pack |

## Evidence Summary

- Chargebacks: Stripe says merchants have a limited response window, must match evidence to dispute categories, and usually get one final submission opportunity. TechRadar coverage of Mastercard-sponsored Datos Insights research reported global businesses were set to lose $15B to fraudulent chargebacks in 2025.
- Returns: Axios reported returns cost retailers about $890B in 2024, with annual return rate near 17%, and that improving return experience was a key 2025 retailer goal.
- Accessibility alt text: The European Commission describes the European Accessibility Act as improving access to accessible products and services; W3C says images need text alternatives based on purpose and context.
- Subscription cancellation: The Guardian reported the FTC click-to-cancel rule was vacated in July 2025 but subscription cancellation complaints remained material; 2026 reporting also shows subscription fatigue and unused subscriptions remain current consumer pressure.

## Why These Fit The "Single Product" Constraint

Each MVP returns one specific artifact:

- Chargeback Evidence Kit: evidence checklist plus dispute counterargument.
- ReturnReply Pro: return triage queue plus customer replies.
- AltText Cataloger: alt text CSV plus gap flags.
- ChurnSave Script: cancellation-save scripts plus confirmation copy.

None requires account management, integrations, payment flows, or multi-user workflows for the first paid delivery.
