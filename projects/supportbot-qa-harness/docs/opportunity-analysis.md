# SupportBot QA Harness Opportunity Analysis

Support teams are adopting AI agents, but hallucinated policy promises and bad handoffs create refunds, escalations, and trust loss.

## Why It Can Sell Quickly

- The buyer already worries about bot mistakes.
- The first project can be scoped to 50 high-value scenarios.
- Recurring QA is easy to justify after policy or knowledge-base changes.

## MVP Scope

- Scenario CSV input
- Required keyword and forbidden phrase checks
- Pass rate and critical gap scoring
- Exportable issue queue
- Client-ready QA report

## Expansion

Add API-based bot conversations, screenshot evidence, scenario libraries by vertical, and scheduled regression reports.
