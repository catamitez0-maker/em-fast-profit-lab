# Opportunity Analysis: InvoiceNudge

Late payment is a persistent small-business pain. Xero-related reporting in 2025 said half of payments to Australian small businesses were late, costing small businesses $1.1B annually there. UK Xero coverage also reported late payments stretching beyond agreed terms and pressuring smaller firms.

## Fast Wedge

Sell weekly receivables follow-up as a productized service to freelancers, agencies, consultants, and contractors. The MVP avoids accounting integrations at first: paste/export invoice rows, produce the queue, send reminders.
