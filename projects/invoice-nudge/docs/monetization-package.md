# Monetization Package: InvoiceNudge

## Positioning

InvoiceNudge helps small service firms turn overdue invoices into a polite weekly follow-up queue. It is positioned as operational support, not legal collections.

## Buyer

- Freelancers, agencies, studios, consultants, bookkeepers, contractors, and project-based service firms.
- Owners who manually rewrite awkward payment emails.
- Bookkeepers that want a lightweight client-facing AR status deliverable.

## Outreach

## DM Version

Do you want a cleaner way to chase unpaid invoices without sounding harsh? I can turn your invoice export into a reminder queue with ready-to-send messages.

## Discovery Questions

- How many unpaid invoices are open right now?
- What tone fits your brand: friendly, concise, or firm?
- Do you want reminders drafted only, or copied into email drafts too?
- Who approves payment follow-ups before they go out?

## Delivery Workflow

1. Collect client, invoice number, due date, amount, and current status.
2. Generate the aging dashboard and reminder queue.
3. Review the top urgent messages with the owner.
4. Export CSV and provide copyable reminders.
5. Offer weekly AR queue maintenance.

## Guarantee

If the setup does not produce a usable reminder queue in the first pass, revise the tone and categories once at no charge.

## Upsell Trigger

Offer the monthly plan after the owner asks for weekly review, wants multiple tones, or introduces a bookkeeper/admin to the process.
