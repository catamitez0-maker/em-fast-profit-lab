# AI Search Presence Monitor Opportunity Analysis

Search behavior is shifting toward AI-generated answers and zero-click surfaces. Businesses need to know whether they appear in those answers and what content makes them more citeable.

## Why It Can Sell Quickly

- SEO buyers already understand ranking reports, but AI answer visibility is still under-instrumented.
- The first deliverable can be made manually from a small prompt sample.
- Monthly tracking is natural because AI answers and cited sources change.

## MVP Scope

- Prompt matrix input
- Presence, position, sentiment, competitor, and citation fields
- Visibility score
- Prioritized content backlog
- Client-ready monthly brief

## Expansion

Add scheduled prompt runs, screenshots, source extraction, competitor trend charts, and agency white-label exports.
