# Opportunity Analysis: MenuMargin Lab

Independent restaurants face a pricing ceiling. Axios coverage of the James Beard Foundation/Deloitte 2026 industry report says broad price hikes and delivery expansion are losing effectiveness, while cost pressure remains. A small operator does not need a complex BI stack first; they need a quick menu math reset and a promo plan.

## Fast Wedge

Sell a $149 menu margin audit to cafes, bars, food trucks, and independent restaurants. The owner sends a menu export or spreadsheet; the tool flags items above target food cost, suggests prices, and generates staff/promo copy.
