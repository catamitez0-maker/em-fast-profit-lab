# Monetization Package: MenuMargin Lab

## Positioning

MenuMargin Lab helps independent restaurants find underpriced menu items and produce simple pricing/promo actions without buying a full restaurant analytics platform.

## Buyer

- Independent restaurants, cafes, bars, food trucks, bakeries, and fast-casual operators.
- Operators with item-level food costs but no easy way to prioritize price changes.
- Restaurant consultants who need a repeatable audit deliverable.

## Outreach

I help independent restaurants find underpriced menu items without guessing. Send 10 menu items with food cost and I will return a sample margin audit.

## DM Version

I noticed you have a strong menu but a lot of operators are hitting a pricing ceiling. I can run a quick margin audit on 10-20 items and send specific price/promo suggestions. Want a sample format?

## Discovery Questions

- Do you track food cost by menu item today?
- Which categories are hardest to price: entrees, drinks, specials, desserts?
- What target food-cost percentage do you use?
- Are there items you want to promote instead of discount?

## Delivery Workflow

1. Collect item, category, price, food cost, and weekly sold count.
2. Generate the audit and sort by high-volume margin risk.
3. Review price suggestions for customer sensitivity and menu psychology.
4. Deliver an owner brief, staff note, and weekly promo copy.
5. Offer weekly ops plan for special menus and seasonal updates.

## Guarantee

If the owner does not find at least 3 actionable items in the audit, add 10 more items at no charge.

## Upsell Trigger

Offer recurring support when the owner changes menus weekly, runs specials, or asks for another batch after food-cost changes.
