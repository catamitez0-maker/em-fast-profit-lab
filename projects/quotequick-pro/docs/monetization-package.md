# Monetization Package: QuoteQuick Pro

## Positioning

QuoteQuick Pro helps home-service contractors answer leads faster with clear scope, price expectations, and follow-up copy. The first product is a service-backed setup, not a self-serve SaaS subscription.

## Buyer

- Owner-operated HVAC, plumbing, roofing, landscaping, electrical, and remodeling companies.
- Local contractors with lead forms but inconsistent follow-up.
- Small agencies that manage marketing for trades businesses.

## DM Version

Noticed your site has a quote form. I built a small quote-response pack for {{trade}} leads: scope, estimate range, SMS, email, and follow-up. Want me to send a sample for one common job?

## Discovery Questions

- What are the 5 service calls you quote most often?
- What hourly rate or minimum visit fee should draft estimates use?
- Where do leads come from: website, Google, paid ads, referrals, marketplaces?
- What part of follow-up is slowest today?

## Delivery Workflow

1. Collect trade, city, service list, base rate, minimum fee, markup, and terms.
2. Generate 10 quote packs in the app.
3. Review each pack for licensing, warranty, and pricing accuracy.
4. Deliver CSV export plus copy/paste SMS and email messages.
5. Offer monthly pack generation for new seasonal services.

## Guarantee

If the first 10 packs do not save at least one hour of owner/admin time, deliver 5 additional packs at no charge.

## Upsell Trigger

Offer the monthly plan after the customer asks for a second batch, seasonal promotion, or agency/client export.
