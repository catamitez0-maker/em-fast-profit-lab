# Opportunity Analysis: QuoteQuick Pro

## Decision

Build a quote-response pack generator for home-service contractors.

## Evidence

Homeowners are staying in place and dealing with delayed maintenance. Service Experts/Talker Research coverage in June 2026 reported that many homeowners are staying put longer, many have delayed plumbing/electrical/HVAC maintenance, and access to trusted professionals is a top factor that would make maintenance more manageable. Harvard/JCHS coverage reported U.S. home improvement and maintenance spending above $500B into 2026.

## Why It Can Monetize Quickly

Contractors already pay for leads. The first sale is not a new operating system; it is a better first response and cleaner quote pack. Prospects can be found by searching local contractors with slow or vague quote flows.

## MVP

The MVP calculates a draft estimate, flags risk, and generates customer-ready messages.
