## Positioning

StayReply Kit helps short-term-rental hosts protect response time and guest experience with ready-to-send message templates and operations checklists.

## Buyer

- Airbnb hosts with one to five listings.
- Co-hosts who manage guest communication.
- Small property managers and cleaners who need consistent guest instructions.

## Discovery Questions

- What are the top 10 guest questions you answer repeatedly?
- What tone should messages use: warm, concise, premium, family-friendly?
- Which issues happen most often: Wi-Fi, parking, check-in, noise, cleaning?
- Do cleaners or co-hosts need a checklist too?

## Delivery Workflow

1. Collect listing context, house rules, common guest questions, and issue scenarios.
2. Generate template pack in the app.
3. Review for platform policy, local rules, and owner preferences.
4. Deliver copyable messages plus cleaner/ops checklist.
5. Offer monthly tuning for seasonal changes and review replies.

## Guarantee

If the host cannot use at least 8 templates immediately, revise the pack once at no charge.

## Upsell Trigger

Offer per-property tuning when the host adds listings, changes house rules, or wants monthly review replies.
