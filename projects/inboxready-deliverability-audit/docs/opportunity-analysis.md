# InboxReady Opportunity Analysis

Email deliverability remains a direct revenue problem for small senders because authentication, unsubscribe, bounce, and spam complaint rules are hard to monitor consistently.

## Why It Can Sell Quickly

- The pain is concrete: campaigns land in spam or lose revenue.
- The first deliverable is simple: score, blockers, and a prioritized fix list.
- The buyer already has the data in ESP exports and DNS records.

## MVP Scope

- Pasted CSV review of campaigns and sender posture
- Risk scoring for authentication, DMARC, unsubscribe, bounce, complaint, and warmup issues
- Exportable audit CSV
- Client-ready report text

## Expansion

Recurring monitoring can add pre-launch checks, monthly trend reporting, ESP migration support, and agency white-label audits.
