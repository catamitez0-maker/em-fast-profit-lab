# AI Disclosure Register Kit Opportunity Analysis

Generative AI content use is growing faster than small teams' governance processes. Many teams need a practical inventory and labeling workflow before formal compliance programs exist.

## Why It Can Sell Quickly

- The buyer has content volume and regulatory uncertainty.
- The first deliverable is concrete: inventory, label queue, and disclosure copy.
- Recurring refresh is natural because campaigns and content calendars change every month.

## MVP Scope

- Content inventory CSV
- AI-use, review, label, and region fields
- Readiness score
- Label queue
- Exportable register and policy pack

## Expansion

Add CMS scans, asset approval workflows, regional rule mapping, evidence attachments, and agency reporting.
